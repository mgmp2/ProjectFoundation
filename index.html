Description

Hallie Gray draws inspiration for her textile designs from her travels around the world. Now you can take her designs with you on your own global adventures. Made of sturdy cotton canvas with a layer of water-resistant vinyl, the Navy Dot Toiletry Bag is both practical and beautiful. It is hand-block printed by artisans in Rajasthan, India, and topped off with a hand-tied, orange tassel. Just looking at it inspires instant wanderlust.

  • Inside/Outside layers made of 100% thick Cotton Canvas, with a water resistant vinyl lining in between
  • Hand-tied tassel made by a 4th generation tassel-tying family in Rajasthan
